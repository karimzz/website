What Is MSP Team?

MSP Team MU is a non-profit scientific organization, was
founded in 2015 in Computer Science Faculty at
Mansoura University by passionate students with
the main purpose of sharing their knowledge and
experience in order to enrich the computer science
community with the necessary skills.
